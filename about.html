About

A snapshot of a ski mountaineering trip, the best form of backcountry recreation.

This project is created by Thomas Kakatsakis. I am from Boulder Colorado and live in Seattle Washington. This project encorporates many topics I am passionate about including: backcountry recreation, avalanche safety, climate change, mapping, and spatial analysis.

This site is created as part of an independent research project for the senior year of my undergraduate degree at the University of Washington. It is conducted under the mentorship of Dr. Suzanne Withers, and has been supported by the help of several faculty members and peers.

Discussion of avalanche accidents and fatalities is conducted with the utmost respect for the friends and family of the deceased. This section of the research is intended to learn from tragedy and better understand how it can be prevented in the future.
